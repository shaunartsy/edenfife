Our Purpose
About Us – Eden Fife Healthcare Services
At Eden Fife Healthcare Services, we are dedicated to supporting older people to live safely, independently, and with dignity in their own homes and communities across Fife. Our work is guided by a strong commitment to delivering compassionate, person-centered care that enhances wellbeing and promotes independence, in line with Scotland’s Health and Social Care Standards, which emphasize dignity, respect, compassion, and positive outcomes for individuals receiving support.
We proudly provide a range of high-quality, regulated services — including care at home, support services, housing support, and live-in care — designed to meet the unique needs, preferences and aspirations of each person we support. These types of services are regulated and overseen by the Care Inspectorate, which ensures that care providers across Scotland meet required safety and quality standards.
Our commitment is strengthened by a dedicated team of experienced healthcare professionals, including Registered Nurses from both Adult and Mental Health backgrounds, skilled Support Workers, and compassionate Carers. As a regulated service, we work in alignment with the Care Inspectorate’s expectations around safe staffing, quality assurance, and continuous improvement — ensuring we deliver reliable, person-centered support that helps older people live well at home and within their local communities.
Our service includes:
Care at Home
We provide tailored personal care and daily living support to help older people stay comfortable, confident, and independent at home. This includes personal care, medication prompts, meal preparation, mobility support, and wellbeing-focused routines — all delivered with dignity and respect.
Support Services
Our community-based support helps reduce loneliness, improve wellbeing and maintain independence. Whether it’s help with shopping, attending appointments, social activities, or companionship, we ensure every person feels valued and connected.
Housing Support
We offer practical assistance to help older people manage their homes safely and comfortably. From maintaining routines to making sure essential needs are met, our housing support services promote stability, independence, and security.
Live-In Care
For individuals who need round-the-clock reassurance, our live-in care service provides continuous support from a trusted, compassionate carer. This ensures safety, comfort, and companionship always — without leaving the familiarity of home.
These services reflect the types of regulated support the Care Inspectorate oversees, including care at home and housing support services across Scotland
Service Locations
Where support is delivered from more than one address, Eden Fife Healthcare Ltd will inform the Care Inspectorate of any changes to service delivery locations.
Staffing Structure
The support service is delivered by one dedicated staff team, based in Glenrothes, ensuring consistency, accountability, and continuity of care.
