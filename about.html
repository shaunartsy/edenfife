About Eden Fife Healthcare

Supporting Older People to Live
Well at Home Across Fife

At Eden Fife Healthcare Services, we are dedicated to supporting older people to live safely, independently, and with dignity in their own homes. Our work is guided by Scotland’s Health and Social Care Standards, ensuring every person receives care that is compassionate and responsive.

  • Dignity & Respect – every person matters.
  • Independence – supporting people to live life their way.
  • Trust & Safety – dependable and professional support.
