Build Your Career with
Eden Fife Healthcare

At Eden Fife Healthcare, we believe that providing outstanding care starts with supporting our team. We are always looking for compassionate, dedicated individuals who want to make a real difference in the lives of older people across Fife. If you are passionate about helping others maintain their dignity and independence, we want to hear from you.

  • Community Support Worker / Caregiver

    As a Community Support Worker, you will be the heart of our service, delivering person-centred care that empowers our clients to live safely and comfortably in their own homes.

    Key Responsibilities:

    • Assisting with daily living activities, including personal care and mobility support.
    • Supporting clients with meal preparation, light household tasks, and medication administration.
    • Providing companionship and emotional support to prevent isolation and loneliness.
    • Observing and reporting any changes in a client's health or wellbeing.
    • Maintaining accurate care records in accordance with Care Inspectorate standards.

    Requirements:

    • A genuine passion for caring for older people with empathy and respect.
    • Excellent communication skills and the ability to build trusting relationships.
    • Reliability, patience, and a positive, problem-solving attitude.
    • A valid driver's license and access to a reliable vehicle (preferred, as travel across Fife is required).
    • Previous care experience is beneficial, but full training and support will be provided for the right candidate.
